Overview

A GCKCastChannel is used to send and receive messages that are tagged with a specific namespace. In this way, multiple channels may be multiplexed over a single connection to a Cast device.

Subclasses should implement the @link GCKCastChannel#didReceiveTextMessage: @endlink method to process incoming messages, and will typically provide additional methods for sending messages that are specific to a given namespace.
